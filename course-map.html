TypeScript 7

TypeScript 7 is not a new-syntax release — it is the compiler, rebuilt. The whole toolchain was ported to Go (the effort known as Project Corsa), running roughly 10× faster while preserving TypeScript 6.0's exact type-checking behaviour. This course is built for developers already writing TypeScript who are moving to the native compiler: what actually changes, what breaks, and how to roll it out. Each module opens a "Refresh your knowledge?" panel for anyone who wants the underlying essentials first.

Leads to the SF Certified TypeScript 7 Practitioner certification.
